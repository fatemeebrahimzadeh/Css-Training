درباره هتل Villa Agrippina Gran Meliá رم

Centrally located in Rome, Gran Meliá Rome Villa Agrippina -The Leading Hotels of the World is within a 15-minute walk of Passeggiata del Gianicolo and Castel Sant'Angelo. This 5-star hotel is 1.4 mi (2.2 km) from Santa Maria in Trastevere and 1.5 mi (2.4 km) from Campo de' Fiori Market.
Relax at the full-service spa, where you can enjoy massages, body treatments, and facials. If you're looking for recreational opportunities, you'll find an outdoor pool, a sauna, and a 24-hour fitness center. This hotel also features complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and supervised childcare/activities.
Enjoy Mediterranean cuisine at Ossimoro, one of the hotel's 2 restaurants, or stay in and take advantage of the 24-hour room service. Relax with a refreshing drink from the poolside bar or one of the 2 bars/lounges. Buffet breakfasts are available daily from 7 AM to 10:30 AM for a fee. Make yourself at home in one of the 116 air-conditioned rooms featuring iPod docking stations and minibars. Your bed comes with down comforters and Egyptian cotton sheets. 32-inch flat-screen televisions with satellite programming provide entertainment, while complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected. Private bathrooms with bathtubs or showers feature rainfall showerheads and designer toiletries.
